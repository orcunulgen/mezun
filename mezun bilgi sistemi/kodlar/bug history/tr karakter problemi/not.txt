tr karakter problemini çözmek adýna

1- proje dosyasý utf8 olsun
2- maven utf 8 ile build etsin

<properties>
		
	<project.build.sourceEncoding>UTF-8</project.build.sourceEncoding>
</properties> 



3- data source da

<property name="jdbcUrl" value="jdbc:mysql://localhost:3306/mezun?characterEncoding=UTF-8" />

url bu þekilde olsun



4- hibernate ayarlarýna bunlarý ekle

<prop key="hibernate.connection.useUnicode">true</prop>
<prop key="hibernate.connection.characterEncoding">UTF-8</prop>
<prop key="hibernate.connection.charSet">UTF-8</prop>
